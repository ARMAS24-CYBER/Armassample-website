SERVICES

We Provide Better

Direct-to-Consumer Sales

Offer fresh, locally-grown vegetables directly to consumers through various channels, such as online orders, farmers' markets, or a farm stand. Home delivery or subscription boxes for regular customers could be a value-added service

Wholesale Supply to Retailers and Restaurants

Partner with local grocery stores, restaurants, and food service providers to supply them with fresh, high-quality hydroponic vegetables. This service could include customized orders based on seasonal demand.

Community-Supported Agriculture (CSA) Programs

Implement a CSA program where customers can subscribe to receive a weekly or monthly box of fresh produce. This service helps build a loyal customer base and ensures consistent revenue.

Hydroponic Vegetable Delivery Service

Offer a convenient delivery service where customers can order fresh vegetables online and have them delivered to their doorsteps. This service can include customizable orders, same-day delivery for local customers, and eco-friendly packaging options to appeal to environmentally-conscious consumers.
